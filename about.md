<section class="about-content">
  <div class="container">
    <p>Same Team is an apparel company based on the idea that we are all in this together, and that people cannot survive independently. Further, it is based on the notion that life is better with friends and that joy and gratitude enhance quality of life.</p>

    <h2>How It Started</h2>
    <p>Same Team originated between co-founders, and partners, Nick &amp; Diana when they would get into a minor argument or squabble. They used this concept to remind each other that their ultimate goal was each other's happiness.</p>

    <h2>The Message</h2>
    <p>This concept has grown to embody our orientation to our friends and family. It helps us remember to be the type of friend that you want to have, or to treat others the way you want to be treated. It reminds us that the ability to give is in fact a gift and that Love is Louder!</p>

    <h2>What We Do</h2>
    <p>We make t-shirts to spread this message. We also create fundraiser shirts where <strong>100% of profits</strong> go directly to support social or environmental causes.</p>
